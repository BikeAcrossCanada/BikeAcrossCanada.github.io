Approved Accommodations Criteria


Place must;

    
  • Be located within 10km of the Network;
  •      Not be a private home;
    •     
  • Allow for 1 night stay;
    •     
  • Have either;
    •         
    OR          

    and have 1 or more of the following attributes;




    - Published special rate for cyclists
    - Have 1 or more amenities for cyclists
    -- Cooking shelter
    -- Tools (Pump, Patch repair kit, Spare parts)
    - Directly recommended by someone in the Facebook group

    Other useful amenities (but not required for Approval) include; Free WiFi, a selection of various phone chargers, a convenience store, drinking water, free (or included) shower, laundry, local tourist guide/ cycling map.
