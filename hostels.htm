Hostels

Hostels are a great way for the budget minded cyclists to experience the culture of the Canadian Town/city.
In Canada, there are 2 main hosteling networks, Backpackers Hostels Canada and Hostelling International Canada. There are other unaffiliated hostels, a selection of them have been added to a Google My Maps Map and Google Maps list, appropriatly labeled as 'Independent Hostels Canada' as it's unaffiliated with the others.
Here's a Google My Maps map for the IHC- Independent Hostels Canada and a shared Google Maps list of these hostels. Many of which are close to the network.
